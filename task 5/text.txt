🔐 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist (For College Students in India)
✅ General Safety Tips
Use strong passwords (mix of letters, numbers, symbols) and avoid using the same password everywhere
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on important accounts like email and banking
Always check the website URL before entering login details (look for HTTPS 🔒)
Avoid clicking on suspicious links from unknown emails, messages, or social media
Keep your phone and apps updated to prevent security vulnerabilities
Do not share personal information (address, ID, passwords) on unknown platforms
💳 Financial / UPI Safety (Important)
Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone — even if they claim to be from the bank
Do not approve unknown payment requests on apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm
Always verify QR codes before scanning — scammers can send fake payment requests
Check transaction details carefully before confirming any payment
🚨 If You Become a Victim
Immediately report the incident on the National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick assistance
Inform your bank and block your card/account if financial fraud occurs
