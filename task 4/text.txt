📋 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students
✅ Do’s
Maintain your privacy settings and review them regularly
Use strong and unique passwords for your accounts
Think before posting anything online
Share positive, informative, and respectful content
Verify information before sharing it
❌ Don’ts
Do not share personal details like phone number, address, or OTP
Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Do not accept friend requests from unknown people
Do not spread fake news or unverified information
